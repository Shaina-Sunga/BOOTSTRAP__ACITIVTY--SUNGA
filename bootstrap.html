About Me

My name is Shaina Faith D. Sunga, and I was born on December 25, 2007, in Minalin, Pampanga, and raised in Manila. Growing up in two vibrant places has helped shape my creativity and passion for the arts.

Among all my talents, singing is my strongest and most cherished skill. Ever since I was young, I’ve loved music and using my voice to express emotion. My teachers and classmates noticed my talent early on, and I was often chosen to represent my school in various singing contests and events. These experiences have helped me grow in confidence and develop my stage presence. Singing is more than just a hobby for me, it’s a way to connect with others and share a part of myself. In addition to singing, I’m also passionate about painting, photography, and cooking. I enjoy painting because it allows me to express emotions and creativity through colors and visuals. Photography lets me capture moments and see beauty in everyday life. Cooking, on the other hand, gives me a sense of joy and accomplishment, especially when I get to share my dishes with friends and family. I consider myself a creative, curious, and expressive person who loves learning new things and growing in different areas. Whether it's through art, music, or food, I always aim to share positivity and inspire others with my passions.
